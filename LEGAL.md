# Authorized Use Only

This repository contains offensive security tooling, methodology notes, and
intentionally vulnerable lab code intended for **authorized security testing and
education only**.

## Acceptable use

You may use the contents of this repository only where you have **explicit,
verifiable authorization** for the specific targets involved, for example:

- A signed penetration-testing engagement or statement of work.
- A bug-bounty program, acting **strictly within its published scope**.
- Systems you own, or a lab environment you control (local VMs, Docker ranges,
  intentionally vulnerable targets such as DVWA / Metasploitable).
- Capture-the-flag events and other sanctioned practice environments.

## Prohibited use

Do **not** use anything here to access, scan, disrupt, or test systems you are
not authorized to test. Unauthorized access to computer systems is illegal in
most jurisdictions (e.g. the US Computer Fraud and Abuse Act, the UK Computer
Misuse Act, and equivalents worldwide) and can carry serious criminal and civil
penalties.

## Your responsibility

- **Stay in scope.** Tools such as `AutoRecon` enforce a scope file, but scope
  enforcement is an aid, not a guarantee. Confirm your authorization and the
  correctness of your scope before every run.
- **Handle findings responsibly.** Scan output, session files, and reports may
  contain sensitive data. Store them securely, share them only with authorized
  parties, and follow responsible-disclosure practice.
